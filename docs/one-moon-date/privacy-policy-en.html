Privacy Policy

One Moon Date

Important: The One Moon Date app does not collect any personal information from users. All data is stored only on the user's device, and no internet connection is required.

1. Information We Collect

The One Moon Date app does not collect any personal information.

  • We do not collect personal identification information such as name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not collect device information such as location, contacts, or photos.
  • No data is transmitted over the internet.
  • No data is stored on or transmitted to servers.

2. Data We Store

The app stores the following information only in the device's internal storage:

  • Language Setting: Selected language (Korean, English, Japanese)
  • Dark Mode Setting: Light/dark mode preference

This information is stored solely to provide app functionality and is kept only on your device. This information is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Data Sharing and Transmission

The One Moon Date app:

  • Does not share data because we do not collect any data.
  • Does not share data with third-party services.
  • Does not use advertising networks or analytics tools.
  • Does not transmit data to servers.

4. Permissions

The One Moon Date app does not request any special permissions.

  • Internet Permission: Not required (app works without internet connection)
  • Location Permission: Not required
  • Contacts Permission: Not required
  • Camera Permission: Not required
  • Storage Permission: Uses only Android default storage (no additional permissions needed)

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The One Moon Date app:

  • Does not use cookies.
  • Does not use user tracking technologies.
  • Does not collect advertising IDs or device IDs.

6. Children's Privacy

Since the One Moon Date app does not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect or process personal information from children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to changes in laws, services, or internal policies. If there are significant changes, we will notify users through app updates.

Updated Privacy Policy will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

8. Your Rights

Although the One Moon Date app does not collect personal information, you can always:

  • Change stored language and dark mode settings in the app settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data.
  • Contact us using the contact information below if you have any questions.
